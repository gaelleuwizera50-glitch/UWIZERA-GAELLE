Code Queen Uganda is a 5-year-old initiative by Educating the Children (ETC) that provides free, intensive training in software development, UI/UX design, and employability skills to young, unemployed Ugandan women. It bridges the gender gap in tech by creating a supportive, women-only environment that has trained over 500 women, aiding them in securing jobs in the tech industry. And am privileged to be a part of this cohort and and am so grateful to our facilitators who are so kind, nice and tirelessly teach us with a lot of patience minus leaving anyone behind despite the fact that sometimes we take long to grasp the concepts.
